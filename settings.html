About This App

Trading Signals Pro uses Smart Money Concepts (SMC) combined with news analysis and trendline analysis to generate high-probability trading signals.

SMC Concepts Explained

  • Order Blocks: Areas where institutions placed large orders. Price often returns to these zones before continuing in the same direction.
  • Fair Value Gaps (FVG): Imbalances in price action shown by gaps between candles. These gaps often get filled as price returns to fair value.
  • Break of Structure (BOS): When price breaks a significant high/low, indicating trend continuation.
  • Change of Character (CHoCH): When price behavior changes, potentially signaling a trend reversal.
  • Liquidity Zones: Areas where stop losses cluster. Institutions often "sweep" these zones before reversing.
  • Market Structure: The pattern of higher highs/lows (uptrend) or lower highs/lows (downtrend).

Understanding Confidence Levels

70-100%: High probability setup with multiple confirmations

50-69%: Moderate probability setup with some confirmations

Below 50%: Low probability setup, trade with caution

News Analysis

The app analyzes recent news to determine currency strength and volatility. Stronger currencies tend to appreciate against weaker ones.

Risk Management

All signals include Stop Loss (SL) and Take Profit (TP) levels based on market structure. The tracker automatically monitors your active signals.
